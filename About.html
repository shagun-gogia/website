About Pro Placement

Built on the foundation of increasing workplace transparency, Pro Placement offers insights into the employee experience powered by millions of company ratings and reviews, CEO approval ratings, salary reports, interview reviews and questions, benefits reviews, office photos and more, combined with the latest jobs. Unlike other career sites, all of this information is shared by those who know a company best — the employees.
In turn, job seekers on Pro Placement are well-researched and more informed about the jobs and companies they apply to and consider joining. This is why thousands of employers across all industries and sizes turn to Pro Placement to help them recruit and hire quality candidates at scale who stay longer.
